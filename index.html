FAQ

Who can signup?

Anyone can! Even though we recommend our programs for middle schoolers, everyone is welcome!

How much do camps cost?

Nothing! All programs are FREE!

What should I bring?

All you need is a computer and fast internet! You can also keep a notebook with you if you would like.

Do I need any past coding experience?

Absolutely not! All of our programs start with the basic fundamentals of every language, so you will learn everything you need.

Any questions, comments, or concerns? Feel free to reach out to us!
